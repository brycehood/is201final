A Little About Me

During my childhood, I loved to play sports with my friends. I loved playing Ultimate Frisbee, Football, Soccer. Other sports that interested me greatly were track, powerlifting, and cross country. Along with all the sports I did, I learned a lot in school and got good grades, enough to be accepted into a prestigous university. After high school, I served a two-year mission for the LDS church in Spain and Andorra. Those two years taught me a lot about myself, and I was able to learn Spanish too! Now, I attend BYU and spend time outside with my friends while I am not studying.
